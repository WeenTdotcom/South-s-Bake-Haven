The Heart Behind The Haven

From a sister’s dream to a thriving local bakery, discover how we started crafting sweet memories in the heart of Tayabas.

A Sweet Beginning

Every great recipe starts with a single ingredient, and for South's Bake Haven, that ingredient was family. Our story began with my sister, who had a passion for baking and a dream of sharing her creations with the world. In the beginning, it was all about the Cheesecake. It was her signature—rich, creamy, and made with so much love that it quickly became a favorite among family and friends.

Passing the Whisk

As time went on, I found myself drawn into the kitchen, inspired by her dedication and the joy her treats brought to people. What started as helping out turned into a deep-seated passion of my own. I took over the reins of the business, determined to keep that sweet legacy alive while pushing the boundaries of what we could offer.

I spent countless hours experimenting with flour, sugar, and heat. I didn't want to just stop at cheesecakes; I wanted to explore the vast world of baking. Through trial, error, and a lot of taste-testing, I mastered the art of custom cakes, fluffy donuts, and those melt-in-your-mouth cinnamon rolls you see on our menu today.

Where We Are Today

Today, South's Bake Haven is more than just a bakery; it is a testament to growth and local craftsmanship. Located in South Palale, Tayabas City, we pride ourselves on using quality ingredients and a personal touch for every order. Whether it’s a bento cake for a small surprise or a grand custom cake for a milestone celebration, we believe that life truly tastes better after a slice of cheesecake (and everything else we bake!).
